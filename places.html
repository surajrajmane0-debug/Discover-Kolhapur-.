Popular Places to Visit in Kolhapur

Mahalaxmi Temple

One of the most famous and ancient temples, dedicated to Goddess Mahalaxmi.

Rankala Lake

A beautiful lake popular for evening walks, food stalls, and boating.

Panhala Fort

Historic fort offering scenic views and rich Maratha history.

New Palace (Shahu Palace)

A stunning palace museum showcasing royal heritage and artifacts.

Jyotiba Temple

Famous hilltop temple known for its spiritual importance and festivals.

Dajipur Wildlife Sanctuary

Also known as the “Bison Sanctuary,” ideal for nature and wildlife lovers.
